How To Use TkTkDownloader?

I worked on this project to make it easy for you to download your favorite TikTok Videos.

Follow these steps to Download videos from TikTok:

Step 1: Open TikTok and look for the post you want to download.

Step 2: Click on "Share" then copy and paste the post link on the text field above.

Step 3: Click the Download Button to download the video in your device.

Step 4: Give it couple of seconds and it will show you the location of your video in your device and how many seconds it took.

Step 5: On your keyboard click on "Win" + "r" and paste in there the location of your file.
